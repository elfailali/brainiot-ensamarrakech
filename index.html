About The Event

Agriculture is a significant part of the Moroccan and African economies. However, climate change has caused a decline in productivity and crop yields, leading to a reduction in employment opportunities. To address this issue, his majesty King Mohammed VI introduced the "Triple A Initiative" to provide funding for small-scale agriculture and support agricultural projects in Africa. Additionally, the "Green Generation 2020-2030" aims to create a sustainable and resilient agricultural sector that can tackle climate change. BrainX is organizing the BrAInIoT2023 event to attract innovative agriculture 4.0 projects from Moroccan and African youth to enhance efficiency, equity, and environmental sustainability using the latest technologies such as AI and IoT. The event hopes to contribute to the development of the agricultural sector by aligning the "Green Generation 2020-2030" strategy.

BrainX Vision for BrAInIoT's future

BrAInIoT2023 challenge
BrainX is hosting the “BrAInIoT2023 in Agriculture 4.0 ” event with the aim of advancing the application of AI and IoT in agriculture to enhance the sustainability and productivity of the Moroccan agricultural sector. The event aims to address challenges primarily arising from external factors, such as climate change, and to facilitate a forum for the development of innovative solutions.
BrAInIoT2024 challenge
BrainX is set to organize the upcoming event, BrAInIoT2024, which aims to dive into the realm of artificial intelligence and the internet of things in football and sports analytics. The event is scheduled to take place in 2024 as preparation for Morocco's hosting of the African Cup in 2025. The event intends to gather professionals in the football and sports analytics field to present their ideas and projects regarding the application of AI and IoT in the industry.
BrAInIoT2025 challenge
BrainX is planning to organize the upcoming event BrAInIoT2025, which will explore the future of urban mobility, transportation and smart cities by leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoT). The event is aimed at bringing together experts, engineers, entrepreneurs, and students to showcase their innovative solutions for urban mobility using AI and IoT.
BrAInIoT2026 challenge
The upcoming event, BrAInIoT2026, which is supposed to be organized by BrainX, will center on the exploration of the potential of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoT) in healthcare and medical sciences. The main aim of the event will be to encourage innovation and collaboration to improve the healthcare sector in Morocco and beyond.
